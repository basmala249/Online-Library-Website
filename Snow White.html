Snow White

Author: The Brothers Grimm

ISBN (13): 9780736421867

Current State:Available NOW.

Overview:

Step into a world of magic and wonder with Snow White! This timeless tale follows a princess of unparalleled beauty, Snow White, who sparks the envy of her wicked stepmother, the Evil Queen. Consumed by jealousy, the Queen plots to get rid of Snow White, but fate takes a twist, leading Snow White on a daring escape into the heart of a mysterious forest.

There, she finds refuge with a group of lovable dwarfs, forging a heartwarming friendship. But the Queen's reach is long, and she devises a cunning plan to vanquish Snow White once and for all. Will true love prevail, or will the Queen's evil intentions triumph? Dive into the pages of Snow White and discover a captivating story filled with adventure, friendship, and a touch of magic that will leave you enchanted.

Learn how to:

  • The importance of kindness.
  • The power of friendship.
  • Beauty is more than skin deep.
  • Don't trust strangers.
