Aladdin

Author: Antoine Galland

ISBN (13): 978-1787414655

Overview:

Dive into a world of wonder and hidden treasures with Aladdin! This enchanting tale follows a young street urchin named Aladdin, whose life takes a surprising turn when he discovers a magical lamp. Trapped within the lamp resides a powerful genie, ready to grant Aladdin's every wish. Suddenly, Aladdin finds himself on a whirlwind adventure filled with riches, palaces, and a beautiful princess named Jasmine. But lurking in the shadows is the cunning Jafar, who desires the genie's power for his own nefarious purposes.

Will Aladdin use his newfound wishes wisely? Can he overcome Jafar's schemes and win the heart of the princess? Aladdin's story is a timeless coming-of-age tale that teaches valuable lessons about honesty, courage, and the importance of staying true to oneself. It's a captivating read filled with magic, adventure, and a touch of romance, sure to spark the imagination of young readers.

Learn many valuable lessons:

  • Importance of Honesty.
  • Resourcefulness and Wit.
  • True Value Lies Beyond Material Possessions.
  • Courage and Facing Your Fears.
