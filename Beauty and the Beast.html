Beauty and the Beast

Beauty and the Beast

Author: Leprince de Beaumont, Jeanne-Marie

ISBN (13): 9780736421973

Current State:Available NOW.

Overview:

Think you know the story of 'Beauty and the Beast'? Think again!

Immerse yourself in a spellbinding tale of beauty and transformation with Beauty and the Beast. This classic story introduces Belle, a bright and curious young woman who yearns for adventure beyond her provincial village. When her father becomes lost in a mysterious castle, Belle bravely ventures in to take his place. There, she encounters a fearsome Beast, a creature cursed for his past arrogance.

Despite the Beast's imposing exterior, Belle discovers a gentle soul trapped within. As days turn into weeks, an unlikely friendship blossoms between them. Will Belle be able to break the curse with her kindness and compassion? Or will the Beast remain forever imprisoned by his monstrous form? Unveil the secrets within the castle walls and embark on a heartwarming journey of self-discovery, love, and the true meaning of beauty.
